                <td style="vertical-align: top; padding-bottom: 1em; width: 45%;">
                  <h2>
                    <span class="mw-headline" id="I_want_info_about_browsing_or_searching">
                      I want info about 
                      <b>
                        browsing or searching
                      </b>
                    </span>
                  </h2>
                  <p>
                    The 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:FAQ/Readers" title="Wikipedia:FAQ/Readers">
                        Readers' FAQ
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     and our 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:About" title="Wikipedia:About">
                        "About" page
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     contain the most commonly sought information about Wikipedia.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    There is a search box in the upper-right corner of every page with the word "Search" in it. Just type what you are looking for in there and then press 
                    <kbd class="keyboard-key nowrap" style="border: 1px solid #aaa; -moz-border-radius: 0.2em; -webkit-border-radius: 0.2em; border-radius: 0.2em; -moz-box-shadow: 0.1em 0.2em 0.2em #ddd; -webkit-box-shadow: 0.1em 0.2em 0.2em #ddd; box-shadow: 0.1em 0.2em 0.2em #ddd; background-color: #f9f9f9; background-image: -moz-linear-gradient(top, #eee, #f9f9f9, #eee); background-image: -o-linear-gradient(top, #eee, #f9f9f9, #eee); background-image: -webkit-linear-gradient(top, #eee, #f9f9f9, #eee); background-image: linear-gradient(to bottom, #eee, #f9f9f9, #eee); padding: 0.1em 0.3em; font-family: inherit; font-size: 0.85em;">
                      <span class="Unicode">
                        ?
                      </span>
                       Enter
                    </kbd>
                    . See 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Help:Searching" title="Help:Searching">
                        Help:Searching
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     for more details.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    There are other ways to browse and explore Wikipedia articles, many can be found at 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Portal:Contents" title="Portal:Contents">
                        Portal:Contents
                      </a>
                    </b>
                    . Also see our 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:General_disclaimer" title="Wikipedia:General disclaimer">
                        disclaimers
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     for cautions about Wikipedia's limitations.
                  </p>
                </td>

                  <div class="floatright">

                <td style="vertical-align: top; padding-bottom: 1em; width: 45%;">
                  <h2>
                    <span class="mw-headline" id="I_want_to_edit_Wikipedia">
                      I want to 
                      <b>
                        edit
                      </b>
                       Wikipedia
                    </span>
                  </h2>
                  <p>
                    Contributing is easy: see 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Tutorial/Editing" title="Wikipedia:Tutorial/Editing">
                        how to edit a page
                      </a>
                    </b>
                    . For a quick summary on participating, see the 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Plain_and_simple" title="Wikipedia:Plain and simple">
                        Plain and simple
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     guide. The 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Help:Cheatsheet" title="Help:Cheatsheet">
                        Cheatsheet
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     will remind you of basic wiki markup. You can practice editing and experiment in a 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Sandbox" title="Wikipedia:Sandbox">
                        sandbox
                      </a>
                    </b>
                    . Or play 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:The_Wikipedia_Adventure/Portal" title="Wikipedia:The Wikipedia Adventure/Portal" class="mw-redirect">
                        the Wikipedia Adventure
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     to learn to edit in an hour.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Be_bold" title="Wikipedia:Be bold">
                        Be bold
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     in improving articles! When adding facts, please 
                    <a href="/wiki/Help:Introduction_to_referencing/1" title="Help:Introduction to referencing/1">
                      provide references
                    </a>
                     so others may verify them. If you are affiliated with the article subject, please see our 
                    <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Conflict_of_interest" title="Wikipedia:Conflict of interest">
                      conflict of interest guideline
                    </a>
                    .
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    If you're looking for places you can help out, the 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Community_portal" title="Wikipedia:Community portal">
                        Community portal
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     is the place to go.
                  </p>

                <td style="vertical-align: top; padding-bottom: 1em;">
                  <h2>
                    <span class="mw-headline" id="I_want_to_report_a_problem_with_an_article">
                      I want to 
                      <b>
                        report a problem
                      </b>
                       with an article
                    </span>
                  </h2>
                  <p>
                    <b>
                      If you have a problem or question with an article about yourself, a family member, friend or colleague, please add a discussion to the 
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:BLPN" title="Wikipedia:BLPN" class="mw-redirect">
                        Biographies of Living Persons' Noticeboard
                      </a>
                      .
                    </b>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    If you spot a problem with an article, 
                    <i>
                      <b>
                        you
                      </b>
                    </i>
                     can fix it directly, by clicking on the "Edit" link at the beginning of that page. 
                    <a href="#I_want_to_edit_Wikipedia">
                      See the "I want to edit Wikipedia" section of this page for more information.
                    </a>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    If you don't feel ready or qualified to fix the article yourself, please post a message on the article's 
                    <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Talk_page_guidelines" title="Wikipedia:Talk page guidelines">
                      talk page
                    </a>
                    . This will bring the matter to the attention of others who work on that article. There is a "Talk" link at the beginning of 
                    <i>
                      every
                    </i>
                     article page.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    Alternatively you can 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Contact_us_-_Readers" title="Wikipedia:Contact us - Readers">
                        contact us
                      </a>
                    </b>
                    . If it's an article about you or your organization, see 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Contact_us_-_Subjects" title="Wikipedia:Contact us - Subjects">
                        Contact us - Subjects
                      </a>
                    </b>
                    .
                  </p>
                </td>

                  </div>
                </td>
                <td style="vertical-align: top; padding-bottom: 1em;">
                  <h2>
                    <span class="mw-headline" id="I_want_to_create_a_new_article_or_upload_media">
                      I want to 
                      <b>
                        create a new article
                      </b>
                       or 
                      <b>
                        upload media
                      </b>
                    </span>
                  </h2>
                  <p>
                    Check 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Your_first_article" title="Wikipedia:Your first article">
                        Your first article
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     to see if your topic is appropriate, then the 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Article_wizard" title="Wikipedia:Article wizard">
                        Article wizard
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     will walk you through creating the article.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    Once you have created an article, see 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Writing_better_articles" title="Wikipedia:Writing better articles">
                        Writing better articles
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     for guidance on how to improve it and what to include (like reference citations).
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    For contributing images, audio or video files, see the 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Help:Introduction_to_uploading_images/1" title="Help:Introduction to uploading images/1">
                        Introduction to uploading images
                      </a>
                    </b>
                    . Then the 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:File_Upload_Wizard" title="Wikipedia:File Upload Wizard">
                        Upload wizard
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     will guide you through that process.
                  </p>

                      </b>
                    </span>
                  </h2>
                  <p>
                    If 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Special:Search" title="Special:Search">
                        searching Wikipedia
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     has not answered your question (
                    <i>
                      for example, questions like "Which country has the world's largest fishing fleet?"
                    </i>
                    ), try the 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Reference_desk" title="Wikipedia:Reference desk">
                        Reference Desk
                      </a>
                    </b>
                    . Volunteers there will attempt to answer your questions on any topic, or point you towards the information you need.
                  </p>

                <td style="vertical-align: top; padding-bottom: 1em;">
                  <h2>
                    <span class="mw-headline" id="I.27m_stuck">
                      I'm 
                      <b>
                        stuck
                      </b>
                    </span>
                  </h2>
                  <p>
                    <span id="gethelp">
                    </span>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    For your convenience, answers have been recorded for the most 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:FAQ" title="Wikipedia:FAQ">
                        frequently asked questions
                      </a>
                    </b>
                    .
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    You can find also 
                    <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Questions" title="Wikipedia:Questions">
                      where to ask questions or make comments
                    </a>
                    .
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    You can ask questions about using Wikipedia at the 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Help_desk" title="Wikipedia:Help desk">
                        Help desk
                      </a>
                    </b>
                     or at the 
                    <b>
                      <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Teahouse" title="Wikipedia:Teahouse">
                        Teahouse
                      </a>
                    </b>
                    . Volunteers will respond as soon as possible.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    Or 
                    <span class="plainlinks">
                      <a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Special:Mytalk&amp;action=edit&amp;preload=Help%3AContents%2Fhelpmepreload&amp;preloadtitle=Help+me%21&amp;section=new">
                        <b>
                          ask for help on your talk page
                        </b>
                      </a>
                    </span>
                     and a volunteer will visit you there!
                  </p>
                  <p>
                    You can get live help with editing in the 
                    <span class="plainlinks">
                      <b>
                        <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Help/clickthrough" title="Wikipedia:Help/clickthrough">
                          help chatroom
                        </a>
                      </b>
                    </span>
                    .
                  </p>
