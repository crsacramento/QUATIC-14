          <p>
            <b>
              Clifton Beach
            </b>
             or Seaview is a beach in 
            <a href="/wiki/Karachi" title="Karachi">
              Karachi
            </a>
            , 
            <a href="/wiki/Pakistan" title="Pakistan">
              Pakistan
            </a>
             located on the 
            <a href="/wiki/Arabian_Sea" title="Arabian Sea">
              Arabian Sea
            </a>
            . It is one of the neighborhoods of 
            <a href="/wiki/Clifton,_Karachi" title="Clifton, Karachi">
              Clifton
            </a>
            , 
            <a href="/wiki/Saddar_Town" title="Saddar Town">
              Saddar Town
            </a>
            . It was the world's most popular silver-sand beach and health resort during 20th century
            <sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">
              [
              <i>
                <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed">
                  <span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2011)">
                    citation needed
                  </span>
                </a>
              </i>
              ]
            </sup>
             but in 2003 it was affected by an oil spill.
            <sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference">
              <a href="#cite_note-1">
                <span>
                  [
                </span>
                1
                <span>
                  ]
                </span>
              </a>
            </sup>
             The beach has attractions for families and tourists, including beachside horse and camel rides, amusement parks, restaurants, and swimming in the Arabian Sea.
          </p>
          <div class="thumb tright">
            <div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;">
              <a href="/wiki/File:Karachi_-_Dolmen_City_-_Construction_Pix_-_Jan_2009_-_33.jpg" class="image">

              </a>

                <a href="/wiki/Dolmen_Mall_Clifton" title="Dolmen Mall Clifton">
                  Dolmen Mall Clifton
                </a>
                , a Shopping Mall and Commercial High-rise Complex at Clifton Beach.
              </div>
            </div>

          <p>
            A gated residential area called 
            <a href="/wiki/Seaview_Clifton_beach" title="Seaview Clifton beach" class="mw-redirect">
              Seaview Apartments
            </a>
             lies close to a section of Clifton beach known as Seaview beach or simply "
            <b>
              Seaview
            </b>
            ". Further ahead lies 
            <b>
              Darakhshan Villas
            </b>
             which comprises residential townhouses and huts.
          </p>

            <div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;">

          <p>
            Another attraction in this area is the recently constructed 
            <a href="/wiki/Universe_Cineplex" title="Universe Cineplex">
              Cineplex
            </a>
             cinema for the people who reside in DHA and Clifton.
            <sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">
              [
              <i>
                <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed">
                  <span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2011)">
                    citation needed
                  </span>
                </a>
              </i>
              ]
            </sup>
             Hollywood films are mostly screened here along with some selected Bollywood films. The cinemas are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and other entertainment sections.
            <sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">
              [
              <i>
                <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed">
                  <span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2011)">
                    citation needed
                  </span>
                </a>
              </i>
              ]
            </sup>
          </p>
          <p>
            Soon as the sun sets,flood lights come up and the picnic continues till midnight.
          </p>
          <p>
            The Defence Housing Authority is fighting erosion by badding more sand, which may change the shape and features of the beach.
            <sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">
              [
              <i>
                <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed">
                  <span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2011)">
                    citation needed
                  </span>
                </a>
              </i>
              ]
            </sup>
          </p>
          <p>
            Other beaches close to the city include 
            <i>
              <a href="/wiki/Sandspit_Beach" title="Sandspit Beach">
                Sandspit
              </a>
            </i>
            , 
            <i>
              <a href="/wiki/Hawke%27s_Bay_(Karachi)" title="Hawke's Bay (Karachi)" class="mw-redirect">
                Hawke's Bay
              </a>
            </i>
            , and 
            <i>
              <a href="/wiki/Paradise_Point" title="Paradise Point">
                Paradise Point
              </a>
            </i>
             (a 
            <a href="/wiki/Sandstone" title="Sandstone">
              sandstone
            </a>
             rock promontory with a 
            <a href="/wiki/Natural_arch" title="Natural arch">
              natural arch
            </a>
            ), sonehra point, French beach, cape Mount, 
            <a href="/wiki/Manora_Beach" title="Manora Beach" class="mw-redirect">
              Manora Beach
            </a>
            , but Clifton Beach is the most popular picnic destination in Karachi.
            <sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">
              [

          <h2>
            <span class="mw-headline" id="Dolmen_Mall">
              Dolmen Mall
            </span>
            <span class="mw-editsection">
              <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
                [
              </span>
              <a href="/w/index.php?title=Clifton_Beach,_Karachi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Dolmen Mall">
                edit
              </a>
              <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
                ]
              </span>
            </span>
          </h2>
          <p>
            <a href="/wiki/Dolmen_Mall_Clifton" title="Dolmen Mall Clifton">
              Dolmen Mall Clifton
            </a>
             is a Mall in Karachi, Pakistan that is located on Seaview, Clifton. It opened in September 2011 successfully and is known for opening first international brand outlets such as Debenhams, Timberland, Next, Monsoon and Accessorize and first openings of international fast food outlets such as Fatburger and Tutti Frutti Frozen Yogurt as well as ?ölen Istanbul. It also opened a kid's arcade called Sindbad's Underwater World.
          </p>
